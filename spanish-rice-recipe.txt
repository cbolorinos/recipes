Ingredients:
 - 1 cup rice
 - two tbsp oil
 - 1 onion
 - 3 cloves garlic
 - cumin, paprika and salt
 - 1.5 cups water

Directions:
  1. Dice the onions and garlic and put them in a pot with the oil on a low flame. 
  2. When onions are soft, put in the spices, and then the rice.
  3. Let the rice fry with the spices, onions and garlic for 1 minute, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add water, and let sit for 20 minutes.
